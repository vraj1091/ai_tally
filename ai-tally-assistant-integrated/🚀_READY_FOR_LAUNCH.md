## 📊 **Using the Chart Selector**

Every dashboard chart can be switched to any of the 35+ types!

**How to Switch Charts:**

1. Click **"Change Chart Type"** button on any chart
2. Select a category:
   - Bar Charts
   - Line Charts
   - Area Charts
   - Pie & Donut
   - Radar & Radial
   - Scatter & Composed
   - Specialized
3. Click your preferred chart type
4. Chart updates instantly!

**Example:**
- Start with a Bar Chart showing sales
- Switch to Line Chart to see trends
- Switch to Pie Chart to see distribution
- Switch to Radar Chart for multi-dimensional view
- All with ONE CLICK!
